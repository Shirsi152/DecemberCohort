Simple cupcake recipe

Serving 12

15 minutes

Prep 20 minutes

In just 35 minutes bake these delicious vanilla cupcakes. Great for birthdays and celebrations or whenever you want a sweet treat. ;)

This is a quick HTML project in which I am building a simplpe cupcake recipe. This recipe was originally created by bbcgoodfood

Ingredients

  • 110g soften butter
  • 110g caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 110g self-raising flour

For the buttercream

  • 150g softened butter
  • 300g icing sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 tbsp milk food colouring paste of your choice (optional)

Step by step instruction

  1. Ensure oven is preheated to 180C/160C fan/gas 4 and fill all trays with cases.
  2. Using an electric whisk beat 110g softened butter and 110g golden caster sugar together until pale and fluffy then whisk in 2 large eggs, one at a time, scraping down the sides of the bowl after each addition.
  3. Add Â½ tsp vanilla extract, 110g self-raising flour and a pinch of salt, whisk until just combined then spoon the mixture into the cupcake cases.
  4. Bake for 15 mins until golden brown and a skewer inserted into the middle of each cake comes out clean. Leave to cool completely on a wire rack.
  5. To make the buttercream, whisk 150g softened butter until super soft then add 300g icing sugar, 1 tsp vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
  6. Whisk together until smooth (start off slowly to avoid an icing sugar cloud) then beat in 3 tbsp milk.
  7. If wanting to colour, stir in the food colouring now. Spoon or pipe onto the cooled cupcakes.

Preparations

  1. Heat oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4 and fill a 12 cupcake tray with cases.
  2. Ensure electric whisk is ready if available.
  3. Alternatively, you can use a regular whisk.
